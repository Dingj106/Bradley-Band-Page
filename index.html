Hilliard Bradley Marching Band

The Hilliard Bradley Marching Band is a organizatuion that competes.

Once you join, you may want to quit but don't.

Marching band is a place where students learn music and perform a show for an audience.

This is an after school activity with competions on Saterdays.

Rehersals are from 3.30 pm to 6 pm for Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays.

We perform for the football team as well on Fridays when they have games.
